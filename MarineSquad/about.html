ABOUT MARINE SQUAD

An Introduction


The ocean is one of the largest ecosystems on Earth.
It generates half of the oxygen we breathe and
contains more than 97% of the world's water.
It provides at least a sixth of the animal protein we eat.
Human activities have caused a serious impact on marine life.
Pollution and drastic climate changes are
among the major consequences that have triggered
major declines in our marine life.

Mission

To create awareness about the plight of the marine life.
We aim to inspire others to join us in taking care of the marine life.

Vision

We aim to be one of the top inspiring organisations in the world.
We will support marine life projects
and we believe that these projects will grow and
get nurtured under our careto make this world a better place.
